Privacy Policy for Animal Sounds for Kids

Last updated: May 20, 2025

1. Introduction

Animal Sounds for Kids (“we,” “us” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use data when you install and use our mobile application.

2. Data We Collect

  • Advertising Identifier
    We integrate Google AdMob to show banner ads. AdMob collects a non-personal Advertising Identifier on your device to serve ads. We do not collect any personal information such as name, email, location, contacts, or device identifiers beyond the Advertising ID.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To load and display ads.
  • To comply with child-directed ads regulations (COPPA). We have tagged our ads appropriately so that only non-personalized ads are served.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob
    Google’s Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
    AdMob’s Data Use Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

5. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

This app is directed to children under 13. In compliance with U.S. COPPA regulations, we do not collect personal information from children. Ads are non-personalized and tagged for child-directed treatment.

6. No Other Data Collection

We do not collect analytics, location data, audio recordings, photos, or any other personal information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date at the top to reflect any revisions.
