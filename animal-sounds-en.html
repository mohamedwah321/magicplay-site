Animal Sounds For Kids

A fun and interactive educational app designed specifically for young children to explore the world of animals by tapping on pictures and hearing real animal sounds.

The app features a simple and friendly interface that allows kids to easily navigate and play sounds by just tapping the animals.

Includes a wide variety of animals such as lion, cat, duck, horse, monkey, tiger, deer, rooster, and more.

This app helps improve listening skills and sound recognition, and enriches children's vocabulary in a fun and safe environment.
